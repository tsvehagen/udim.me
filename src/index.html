Welcome to udim.me
Dimmer meets design

Usage

Plug in

Connect dimmer box to power outlet and lamp

Turn on

Turn on by facing light side up

Dim

Dim the light by rotating the puck

Turn off

Turn off by facing dark side up

Battery

The puck is powered by a standard CR2032 battery which can easily be changed. Battery lifetime is around 6 months depending on usage.

Communication

The communication between puck and dimmer box is done via Bluetooth Low Energy. The working range is about 10 m depending on environment.

Lamps

Works with incandescent and halogen lamps up to 80 W. Will also work with led lights that can handle leading-edge phase dimming.

Dimensions

Diameter: 11 cm
Height: 3 cm
